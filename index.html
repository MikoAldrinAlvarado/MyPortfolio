About Me

Hello! My name is Miko. I graduated with a degree in Computer Science from the College of Mary Immaculate in Pandi, Bulacan. My journey in technology began with a strong foundation in computer science, where I learned to combine structured problem-solving with creative design.

Throughout my studies and projects, I’ve gained hands-on experience with multiple programming languages and tools, including Python, JavaScript/Node.js, C++, SQL, HTML, and CSS. As I continue my journey, I am eager to contribute my skills to collaborative projects, learn new technologies, and further improve through real-world experience. I believe every opportunity is a chance to grow, help others, and make technology more accessible and I’m excited to bring that mindset into my career.
